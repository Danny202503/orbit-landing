The Front Desk Revolution

This is the true power of our Cloud-Native SPA architecture. The exact moment an external OTA booking (like Agoda) is confirmed, a sleek **Live OTA Reservations** popup instantly materializes dead-center on the screen. True zero overbookings, zero refresh required.
